About This Simulator

Market Economy Simulator V2 is an ambitious business simulation game where you operate a new firm in an established market economy. Compete with AI-controlled firms, manage complex production chains, buy and sell real estate, and maintain GAAP-compliant financial records.

Gameplay Overview

You start as a new firm with limited capital in a functioning market economy. Existing AI firms are already operating at break-even, setting market prices through their trading activity.

Your challenge is to build a profitable business by:

  • Analyzing market conditions and identifying opportunities
  • Purchasing raw materials and intermediate goods
  • Manufacturing products in your production facilities
  • Selling finished goods on the open market
  • Managing cash flow and maintaining financial health
  • Expanding through strategic real estate investments
  • Competing with AI firms for market share

Technical Design

Built with pure vanilla JavaScript, HTML, and CSS with no external dependencies. The architecture features 28+ modular files organized by domain (accounting, economy, entities, real estate, UI), totaling an estimated 3,000-5,000 lines of code.
